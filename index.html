Why Adopt?

Adopting a pet changes two lives — theirs and yours.

❤️

Save a Life

Millions of healthy pets are in shelters waiting for a home. Adopting gives them a second chance they deserve.

💰

Lower Cost

Adopted pets are usually already vaccinated, microchipped, and spayed or neutered — saving you hundreds of dollars.

🏠

Fight Puppy Mills

Adopting instead of buying reduces demand for inhumane breeding operations and helps end their cruelty.

😊

Better for You

Studies show pet owners have lower stress, lower blood pressure, and feel significantly less lonely.

🐾

Know What You're Getting

Shelter staff observe pets daily and can match you with one that fits your lifestyle and personality.

🌍

Help the Community

Every adoption frees up space for another animal in need and supports your local shelter.

Pet Care Tips

Keep your dog or cat happy, healthy, and safe.

🥗

Nutrition

Feed age-appropriate food. Fresh water must always be available. Avoid grapes, chocolate, onions, and xylitol.

🏃

Exercise

Dogs need at least 30 minutes of exercise daily. Cats benefit from interactive play sessions twice a day.

🏥

Vet Visits

Annual check-ups keep your pet healthy and catch problems early. Always keep vaccinations up to date.

🔖

ID & Microchip

Always use a collar ID tag and ask your vet about microchipping — it dramatically improves reunion chances.

🛁

Grooming

Brush your pet regularly. Trim nails monthly. Bathe dogs every 4–8 weeks depending on breed.

🧠

Mental Health

Rotate toys, train new tricks, and give your pet plenty of attention. Boredom leads to destructive behaviour.

About Us

Who we are and why we built FurAndFound.

Our Mission

FurAndFound was built to make sure no pet owner ever feels helpless when their dog or cat goes missing. We give owners a calm, step-by-step plan and a community that rallies around them.

Who Made This

FurAndFound is a Grade 6 class project created by Marcus Kwan. It was built to solve a real problem: first-time pet owners don't know what to do when their pet disappears.

What We Believe

  • Every pet deserves to go home.
  • Information should be simple and calm in a crisis.
  • Communities are more powerful than any single person.
  • Adopting is always better than buying.
